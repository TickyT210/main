1978 Chevy Truck

My dad used to drive one of these. The truck is now rotting in a field in cranbrook BC. The 1978 chevy truck can have a 3 speed automatic or a 3-4 speed manual transmission. My dad says that his truck was probably the loudest car on the road.

1987 Buick Regal GNX

This car is a really nice shape. I love the body-work on this car. The 1987 Buick Regal GNX has a automatic transmission. This car could be a great lowrider or cruiser. This car has power and style on the road.

1970 Dodge Charger

This car is many movies/TV shows. Some of them are "The Dukes of Hazard" or "Fast and Furious". Both Cars have an amazing story to them and are really powerful. The 1970 Dodge Charger has a 4 speed automatic. This car has a nice clean look on the roads

1968 Dodge Dart Hemi super sport

I have a small model of this car. It has a 4 speed automatic with a lot of power. This car is mostly turned into Drag cars. The model that I made has a 6 speed manual but its also very small and doesn't move. This car has a hood scoop which makes the car look so much better.

1972 Corvette Stingray

I love corvettes! these cars are beautiful when they roar past you on the highway. The 1972 Corvette Stingray has a 4 speed manual or a 3 speed automatic. This another one of my dad's favorite cars. The shape of this car is so iconic that it's hard not to tell what it is.

1971 GMC Vandura

Not one of the greates cars in the world but it looks like a nice van. The 1971 GMC Vandura has a 3 speed manual. This is just (in my eyes) the nices looking vans made by GMC but some say there are better looking ones out there.

1970 chevy chevelle Super Sport (SS)

This car is always a sight to see on the road. The sound, the color, the shine and the speed. The 1970 chevy chevelle super sport can have a 4 speed manual or a 3 speed automatic. This car is one of those cars that you would see at most car shows.
